Distributed Systems:
	- Adv:
		- Scalability: by distributing the work accross various machines
		- Availability: failure of part doesn't means system failure.
		- Extendability: multiple teams with multiple technologies can work in parallel 
	- Disadv:
		- Developing distributed system is more costly.
		- Testing the whole system is more difficult.
		- Distributed transactions is a nightmare.
		- Data Synchronization became a problem.
